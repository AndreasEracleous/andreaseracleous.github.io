OUR GOAL

To bring cryptocurrency to the world.

Cryptocurrency is revolutionary new technology which brings an an enormous realm of freedom to money. However, even after 9 years, cryptocurrency adoption is only ~0.5% of the world population. Our goal is to introduce and bring cryptocurrency to the masses.

HOW ENERGI SETS ITSELF APART

ENERGI EMISSION SCHEDULE

Approximately 1 million Energi will be released per month. The allocation can be observed easily as "10/10/40/40."

  • 10% will go to the Energi Backbone
  • 10% to the Miners (which will eventually transition to Proof of Stake)
  • 40% to Masternodes
  • 40% to the Treasury
